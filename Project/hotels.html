Raj palace

The Raj Palace in Jaipur is a magnificent luxury hotel that embodies the opulence and grandeur of Rajasthan's royal heritage. Housed in a historic palace, it offers lavish rooms and suites adorned with exquisite decor, creating an ambiance of regal elegance. Guests can indulge in fine dining, spa treatments, and personalized service amidst the rich architectural beauty of this iconic establishment.

Ram bagh palace

Rambagh Palace in Jaipur is a former royal residence turned luxury hotel, nestled in 47 acres of lush gardens. It exudes timeless elegance and offers opulent rooms and suites with a regal touch. This majestic palace hotel boasts impeccable service, world-class dining, and a rejuvenating spa experience. With its rich history and stunning surroundings, Rambagh Palace is a true haven of luxury and tranquility in the Pink City.

oberoi rajvillas

The Oberoi Rajvilas in Jaipur is a luxurious resort designed like a traditional Rajasthani fort. Set amidst 32 acres of lush gardens, it offers an enchanting blend of modern luxury and Rajput hospitality. Guests can enjoy well-appointed accommodations, exquisite dining options, a world-class spa, and activities like yoga and nature walks. This retreat exemplifies serenity and luxury in the heart of Rajasthan.

Fairmont jaipur

Fairmont Jaipur, located on the outskirts of Jaipur, offers a captivating blend of Mughal and Rajasthani architecture. The resort's regal charm is reflected in its spacious rooms and suites, complete with modern amenities. Guests can unwind with spa treatments, savor diverse culinary delights, and explore the rich heritage of Jaipur. Fairmont Jaipur provides a luxurious and tranquil escape in a picturesque setting.

Jai mahal palace

Taj Jai Mahal Palace in Jaipur is a splendid hotel surrounded by 18 acres of Mughal gardens. This architectural masterpiece seamlessly combines the grandeur of Rajasthan's heritage with contemporary luxury. Guests can enjoy opulent rooms, fine dining at various restaurants, rejuvenating spa experiences, and a glimpse into Jaipur's royal history. The Taj Jai Mahal Palace offers an unforgettable stay in a regal oasis.

samod palace

Samode Palace, located an hour's drive from Jaipur, is a magnificent heritage hotel known for its stunning architecture and timeless charm. This regal retreat offers exquisitely appointed rooms, a tranquil atmosphere, and an opportunity to immerse in Rajasthan's royal history. Guests can savor traditional cuisine, explore the palace's intricate design, and experience the rich cultural heritage of Jaipur during their stay at Samode Palace.
