Metro Map

Jaipur Metro, inaugurated in 2015, serves as a convenient mode of transportation in the Pink City, Jaipur, India. the metro system has two operational lines: Pink Line and Blue Line.

1. Pink Line: Running from Mansarovar to Chandpole, the Pink Line covers 11.69 kilometers and serves as a lifeline for daily commuters.

2. Blue Line: The Blue Line connects Sitapura Industrial Area to Ambabari, spanning approximately 9.63 kilometers.

Jaipur Metro offers a reliable and efficient way to navigate the city's bustling streets and reach popular destinations. However, please verify the latest updates and extensions to the metro network as new lines may have been added since my last update.

Bus Map

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, has a comprehensive bus transport system managed by the Jaipur City Transport Services Limited (JCTSL). The city offers an extensive network of bus routes covering various parts of Jaipur and its suburbs. These routes connect key areas, including residential neighborhoods, commercial districts, and important landmarks. JCTSL operates a fleet of low-floor and semi-low-floor buses, making public transportation more accessible and comfortable for commuters. Additionally, there are different types of buses, including regular buses, mini-buses, and AC buses, providing options for passengers with various preferences and budget constraints. It's advisable to check with local authorities or transportation agencies for the most up-to-date information on bus routes, schedules, and fare structures.
